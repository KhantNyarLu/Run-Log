The Problem

Strava Buries
Your Data.

You log every run. But getting to the actual numbers means scrolling past social stuff that has nothing to do with your training. Kudos, segment leaderboards, friend activity. None of it tells you whether you are ready for your race.

RunLog skips all of that. Connect your Strava account once and your data is just there every time you open it, pulled fresh from the API on every page load.

"Am I running more than last week? Is my pace improving? Is my training mix right for this phase? These questions should not take 30 seconds to answer." The runner RunLog is built for
What you get

Four Charts.
Every Answer.

01
Weekly Mileage
Track whether your total running volume is increasing week over week. The most important question in any training plan.
02
Run Type Split
See if your balance of easy, tempo, long, and interval runs matches your current race prep phase.
03
Pace Per Run
Compare pace across all your runs this year. A shorter bar means a faster pace. Watch your improvement build over time.
04
Distance Badges
Your fastest 5K, 10K, and half marathon pace highlighted automatically. See your personal bests without hunting for them.
